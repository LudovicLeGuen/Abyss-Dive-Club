Discover the deep

Explore and Have fun

Snorkeling

We organize guided tours around the Cannes bay area to enjoy the mediterranean fauna and flora. We encourage you to come with your friends and family to enjoy a fun and memorable activity.
Do not forget the camera!

Scuba Diving

Join the scuba group and discover the Cannes bay area with us. We roam to a maximum depth of 100 ft. We go through wrecks, reefs and much more. Relax and discover a hidden side of Cannes.
You must have your diver certificate and your own equipment to join the fun.

Free diving

Free diving is all about self control and mind peacefulness. We train veterans and guide beginners in this wonderfull journey into the deep. Do not fear the abyss, embrace it, we will be with you all the way.
All ages are accepted so come with your children and parents!
